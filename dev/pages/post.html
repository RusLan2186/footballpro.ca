Inter Milan Signs Spanish Striker Ruiz

Inter Milan has officially announced the signing of Spanish forward Alejandro Ruiz from Sevilla in a deal worth €45 million. The 22-year-old striker has been one of the most promising young talents in La Liga and is now set to make his mark in Serie A.

Ruiz has been a standout performer for Sevilla over the past two seasons, scoring 18 goals in La Liga last year and providing several key assists. Known for his pace, dribbling ability, and clinical finishing, he quickly became one of the most sought-after young forwards in Europe. His performances not only helped Sevilla secure a Champions League spot but also earned him a call-up to the Spanish national team.

Inter Milan has been searching for a dynamic striker to complement their attacking lineup, and Ruiz fits the profile perfectly. The club’s sporting director praised the signing, stating: "Alejandro is a young player with great potential. We believe he can be a key part of our squad and help us achieve our goals this season." With Inter currently battling for the Serie A title and aiming for a deep Champions League run, Ruiz’s arrival is expected to add depth and attacking firepower to the squad.

Ruiz is known for his versatility, capable of playing as a center-forward or on the wings.
